This document establishes the **formal disclaimer and no-reliance framework** governing all GHI publications and outputs.

- prevent any implied duty, warranty, or representation;
- neutralize legal, financial, regulatory, and operational exposure;
- clearly position GHI as a **reference-only, non-actionable framework**.

This notice is **normative**, public, and binding for all users.
