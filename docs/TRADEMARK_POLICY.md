# GHI Trademark Policy

This document describes how the names, brands and logos associated with  
**GHI – Global HashCost Index** may be used.

---

## 1. Protected Elements

The following items are protected by trademark rights:

- The name **“GHI”**
- The name **“Global HashCost Index”**
- The GHI logo and any derivative visual identity
- Abbreviations or visuals confusingly similar to the above

---

## 2. Allowed Uses (without permission)

You may use the GHI name or logo **only** in the following cases:

- Academic research  
- Documentation referencing the GHI standard  
- Integrations that explicitly cite GHI as a data source  
- Non-commercial dashboards that display public GHI values  

You must include:

> “GHI is an open standard maintained by the GHI Standard group.”

---

## 3. Uses Requiring Explicit Permission

The following uses require prior written authorization:

- Creation of commercial dashboards or analytics products  
- Repackaging GHI data as a proprietary dataset  
- Using the GHI name to describe another index or methodology  
- Creating logos or visuals similar to GHI's identity  
- Any impression of official endorsement  

Requests: `legal@ghi-standard.org` (ou adresse à définir)

---

## 4. Forbidden Uses

You may **not**:

- Claim ownership of the GHI trademark  
- Suggest GHI endorses your product if this is not the case  
- Use the logo in a way that alters its meaning  
- Use the term “GHI-compliant” without following the full specification

---

## 5. License Compatibility

GHI code is under the **MIT License**,  
but trademarks are **NOT open-source**.

Open-source ≠ droit d’utiliser la marque sans contrôle.

---

## 6. Enforcement

The GHI Standard group may revoke permissions in case of:

- misuse  
- misleading communication  
- trademark dilution  

This protects the integrity and reliability of the GHI ecosystem.
